Standard Curriculum
Kinder (3-6) Years

Kindergarten for ages 3-6 focuses on early childhood development through play-based and structured activities.

It helps children build social, emotional, cognitive, and physical skills.

Elementary School

Elementary school typically providing foundational education in reading, writing, math, science, and social studies .

It fosters critical thinking, creativity, and teamwork through engaging lessons, hands-on activities, and group projects.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school bridges childhood and adolescence, focusing on academic growth and personal development.

It introduces more specialized subjects like advanced math, science, history, and literature.
